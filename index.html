Who are we?

We are an HCI research lab, located in Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD) in Singapore. Our research interests are broad but we tend to focus on interaction with mobile and wearable devices, assistive technologies, virtual & augmented reality. The lab was founded by Kenny Choo and Simon Perrault, faculties in the Information Systems Technology and Design (ISTD) pillar of SUTD.
